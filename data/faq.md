# Sample FAQs

Replace the entries below with your own. Each `## ` heading starts a new
FAQ chunk that the bot can retrieve and cite.

## How do I reset my password?
Go to https://aka.ms/passwordreset and follow the prompts. If you are still
locked out after 15 minutes, contact the IT helpdesk at helpdesk@example.com.

## What are the support hours?
The helpdesk is staffed Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Pacific Time.
For urgent after-hours issues, page the on-call engineer via PagerDuty.

## How do I request a new laptop?
Open a ticket in ServiceNow under "Hardware Request". Standard approval
takes 2–3 business days. Managers can expedite by emailing it-ops@example.com.

## Where can I find the employee handbook?
The latest handbook lives in SharePoint at
https://contoso.sharepoint.com/sites/hr/handbook. It is updated quarterly.
